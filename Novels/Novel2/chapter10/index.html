Ummide.

Kuch waqt ke baad dard bhi thak jaata hai.

Aur phir dil ke kisi kone se dheere-dheere umeed janam lene lagti hai.

Main pehli baar bina bojh ke saans le paaya.

Uska khayal ab bhi aata tha, par ab woh chubhta nahi tha.

Jaise yaadein bhi samajh gayi ho ki ab aage badhna zaroori hai.

Ek subah jab sooraj nikla, toh laga zindagi phir se kuch kehna chahti hai.

Main us roshni ko pehli baar rokna nahi chahta tha.

Shayad pyaar sirf kisi ek se nahi hota,

kabhi-kabhi woh khud se bhi hota hai.

Khud ko samajhna, khud ko maaf karna â€” yeh bhi ek mohabbat hi hai.

Main ab bhi kahani ka hissa hoon,

par kahani ab sirf uske naam ki nahi rahi.

Zindagi ke naye panne dheere-dheere khul rahe the.

Ummidein kabhi shor nahi karti,

bas chup-chaap dil me jagah bana leti hain.

Aur phir ek din andhere ko halka kar deti hain.

Mujhe nahi pata aage kya likha hai,

par itna zaroor jaanta hoon ki ab darr kam hai.

Shayad isi ka naam ummeed hota hai.
