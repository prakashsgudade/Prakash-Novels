Chapter 12 – Akhari Ehsaas

Shaam dheere-dheere raat mein badal rahi thi. Aasmaan pe chaand poora nahi tha, par jitna tha, utna hi kaafi lag raha tha. Jaise zindagi bhi — poori nahi, par jitni mili, utni mein hi sab kuch tha.

Woh akela khada tha. Haath mein phone tha, screen band. Us number ko dekhe bina hi use mehsoos ho raha tha. Kuch rishte awaaz ke mohtaaj nahi hote, bas ehsaas hi kaafi hota hai.

“Agar tum yahan hoti…” yeh jumla uske hothon tak aaya, par poora kabhi hua hi nahi. Kyunki kuch baatein adhoori rehkar hi zyada sach lagti hain.

Usne yaadon ko yaad ki tarah nahi, zakhm ki tarah sambhal kar rakha tha. Har yaad dard deti thi, par wahi dard use zinda hone ka saboot bhi deta tha.

Usne samjha tha ki waqt sab theek kar deta hai. Par waqt sirf aadat badalta hai, yaadein nahi.

Kuch log zindagi se chale jaate hain, par dil ke kamre mein reh jaate hain — bina kiraya diye.

Aaj pehli baar usne khud se sach bola — “Main theek hoon… par poora nahi.”

Aur shayad poora hona zaroori bhi nahi hota. Kuch kahaniyaan adhoori rehkar hi amar ho jaati hain.

Usne phone uthaya, screen unlock ki. Naam nahi tha, bas ek number tha.

Ungli call button par ruki. Phir usne phone band kar diya. Kyunki kuch awaazon ko yaadon mein hi rehne dena behtar hota hai.

“Agar kabhi hum milte…” is jumle ke aage usne hamesha khamoshi rakhi.

Kyunki har sawaal ka jawaab milna zaroori nahi hota. Kuch sawaal hi kahani ban jaate hain.

Raat gehri ho chuki thi. Usne aakhri baar aasmaan ki taraf dekha aur muskuraya.

Yeh thi uski ankahi dastan — na poori, na khatam, bas ek akhari ehsaas… jo hamesha dil mein zinda rahega.
