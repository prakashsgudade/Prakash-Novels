Chapter 12 – Akhari Ehsaas

Har kahani ka ek aakhri ehsaas hota hai, jo khatam hone ke baad bhi saath reh jaata hai.

Woh jaanta tha ki yeh mulaqat aakhri hai, par usne kuch kaha nahi. Kuch baatein kehne se zyada, mehsoos karne ke liye hoti hain.

Usne uski aankhon mein dekha, wahan shikayat nahi thi, bas ek adhoori si muskurahat thi.

“Khush rehna,” bas yahi dua dono ne bina kahe de di.

Jab woh door hui, toh laga jaise kahani ruk gayi ho. Par asal mein, wahi kahani ka sabse gehra hissa tha.

Kuch pyaar mil kar poore hote hain, aur kuch bichhad kar amar. Unka pyaar shayad doosra wala tha.

Yeh thi Ankahi Dastan — jo shabdon mein nahi, sirf ehsaason mein zinda rahegi.
