Ankahi Dastan

Chapter 2 ‚Äì Ankhahe Shabd

Ankhahe shabd.

Kuch baatein aisi hoti hain jo zubaan tak aate-aate chup ho jaati hain.

Dil unhe mehsoos karta hai, par alfaaz unka saath nahi dete.

Uske saamne khade hoke main kai baar bolna chahta tha,

par har baar lafz kahin kho jaate the.

Jaise woh sab kuch samajh leti ho, bina sune.

Woh muskurati thi, aur main chup reh jaata tha.

Shayad meri khamoshi hi mere jazbaat bol rahi thi.

Kai baar socha, ek hi jumla keh doon,

par darr tha kahin woh pal toot na jaaye.

Isliye baatein reh gayi dil ke kone me band.

Ankhahe shabd aksar sabse bhaari hote hain.

Kyunki unme umeed bhi hoti hai aur darr bhi.

Woh jab paas hoti thi, duniya thodi si khamosh lagti thi.

Aur jab door jaati thi, mere shabd aur zyada bojhil.

Mujhe nahi pata kab keh paunga main,

par itna yaqeen hai ki yeh ankhahe shabd Ankahi Dastan ka hissa ban chuke hain.
