Chupa Dard.

Kuch dard bolte nahi hain,

bas dil ke andar chup-chaap rehte hain.

Muskan chehre par hoti hai,

par uske peeche ek poori kahani chhupi hoti hai.

Log samajhte hain sab theek hai,

kyunki hum shikayat nahi karte.

Raat ko jab khamoshi hoti hai,

tab yeh chupa dard awaaz ban jaata hai.

Aankhon ke aansu aksar bahar nahi aate,

dil ke andar hi sookh jaate hain.

Har insaan kuch na kuch chupaye phirta hai,

bas fark itna hai koi dikha deta hai, koi nahi.

Shayad isi liye,

Ankahi Dastan sirf likhi nahi jaati,

mehsoos ki jaati hai.
