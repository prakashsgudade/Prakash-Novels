Adhuri Mulakat.

Kuch mulakaatein poori hoke bhi adhoori reh jaati hain.

Woh saamne thi,

par kehne ko bahut kuch reh gaya.

Nazrein mili,

par alfaaz khamosh rahe.

Waqt ne jaldi ki,

aur hum dheere pad gaye.

Haath mil sakte the,

par faasle beech me aa gaye.

Shayad dar tha,

ya shayad kismat adhuri hi likhi thi.

Aur phir samajh aaya,

kuch mulakaatein yaadon ke liye hoti hain,

poori hone ke liye nahi.
