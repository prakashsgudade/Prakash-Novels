Adhuri Dastan.

Har kahani ka anth hota hai, par kuch kahaniyaan bas dheere se ruk jaati hain.

Na poori tarah khatam, na hi poori tarah zinda.

Hum dono ek hi shehar me rahe, par jaise alag-alag zindagiyan jeene lage.

Kabhi saamne aa jaate, toh muskurahat reh jaati, baat nahi hoti.

Main aksar sochta raha,

agar thoda sa aur bol liya hota, thoda sa aur ruk gaya hota,

toh shayad kahani ka rang kuch aur hota.

Par zindagi â€œshayadâ€ par nahi chalti.

Woh apni raah chunti hai, aur hum bas yaadein samet lete hain.

Aaj bhi jab kabhi akela hota hoon, uska khayal aa jaata hai.

Dard ke saath nahi, balki ek ajeeb si shanti ke saath.

Shayad pyaar ka matlab paana hi nahi hota,

kabhi-kabhi kisi ko dil me zinda rakhna bhi kaafi hota hai.

Aur is tarah Ankahi Dastan adhuri hi sahi,

par sachchi ban kar mere saath chalti rahi.

Shayad isi ka naam mohabbat hota hai.
